# Planning & Kanban Workflow Decomposition

Analysed assigned Kanban work items to identify hidden dependencies and determine when tasks needed to be split into smaller sub-tasks, improving delivery clarity, sequencing, and execution planning.

## Approach

1. **Work item analysis** — Review assigned Kanban items for scope and implicit dependencies
2. **Dependency identification** — Surface hidden blockers and cross-team dependencies
3. **Task decomposition** — Split oversized items into deliverable sub-tasks
4. **Sequencing** — Order sub-tasks to respect dependencies and enable parallel work

## Outcomes

- Improved delivery clarity through right-sized work items
- Better sequencing of dependent tasks
- Reduced risk of blocked work mid-sprint
- Clearer execution planning for the team
