# AI-Enabled Engineering & Workflow Automation

Ever since organistions enabled teams to adopt AI tools as part of their workflows to improve productivity or create products. One thing remains, a human in the loop is required to tackle issues such as 

- Security 
- Verifications 
- etc etc

Over time using AI, we realised that without context, everyone is just spending tokens using AI to solve problems that otherwise could be solved by a re-usable workflow. 

e.g IaC & automtion have been a significant pillar as part of the DevOps practices. Each day ou spend in an organisation is time you are spending gaining more context about your organisaton, your team processes etc.

As we evolved as a team, each one of us has adopted these AI tools to help us improve productivity in various ways. In this repo, I will be documenting my journey and try to capture every bit of detail that wil help someone out there understand the tradeoffs.
